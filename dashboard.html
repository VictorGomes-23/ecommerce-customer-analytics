Dashboard Features

Overview Module

8 real-time KPI cards tracking revenue, customers, orders, AOV, churn rate, and more. Dynamic calculations update based on filter selections.

  • Monthly revenue trend visualization
  • Top 10 products bar chart
  • Customer segment distribution
  • Geographic revenue analysis

Customer Segments

Deep dive into RFM segmentation with interactive tables and charts showing segment performance metrics.

  • Segment performance summary table
  • RFM score distribution histograms
  • Revenue contribution pie chart
  • CLV box plots by segment

Churn Analysis

Real-time churn risk assessment with ML predictions identifying high-risk customers and revenue at risk.

  • High-risk customer count and revenue
  • Churn probability distribution
  • Risk category breakdown
  • Top 20 highest risk customers table

Product Performance

Comprehensive product analytics with interactive tables and charts for sales analysis.

  • Top 20 products by revenue
  • Sortable product performance table
  • Units sold and pricing metrics
  • Search and filter capabilities

Cohort Analysis

Interactive retention heatmap tracking customer cohorts over time with hover details.

  • Cohort retention heatmap
  • Average retention curves
  • Interactive hover tooltips
  • Month-over-month tracking

Global Filters

Dynamic filtering across all modules for customized analysis and drill-down capabilities.

  • Date range selector
  • Customer segment filter
  • Country multi-select
  • Real-time updates across all tabs
